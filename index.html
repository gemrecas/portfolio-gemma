Hello! my name is Gemma

I’M AN ILLUSTRATOR who lives in Barcelona. I hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree from the Polytechnic University of Valencia and a Master’s Degree from the Higher School of Art and Technology, also in Valencia. I am currently training in WEB DESIGN, and I work between Illustration, Photography, Design, and anything that I can use to achieve good results with my projects. If you are curious or have any questions about my work or myself, please do not hesitate to contact me. The softwares I work with are Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, and I’m currently training with other ones, such as Sketch.

I consider myself to be a versatile and adaptable person; I can comfortably work in group projects and I’m able to manage my own ones by myself. Among my interests are photography –I have a good command of photograph skills, be it outdoors, portrait or packshot- animation, packaging, nature, music, cycling, and videogames.
